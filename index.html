Welcome to Capstone Cloud Services

Capstone Cloud Services are an SME cloud service provider. The aim of the business is to provide personal and corporate level cloud services to users who have little to no understanding of the cloud.

The company provides three main services: Storage, Web Hosting, and Bespoke packages. The storage service allows users (either personal or corporate) to securely store all types of documents in the cloud. The service is powered by AWS S3. The service is similar to Dropbox or Google Cloud.
